## Regional Safety & EMC Certifications

Topleo products are tested and certified to the safety, electromagnetic compatibility (EMC), and radio frequency (RF) standards required for sale in all major global markets. The table below summarizes available regional certifications across the 2026 lineup.

| Certification | Region | Scope |
|---|---|---|
| **CE** | European Union | Safety (LVD), EMC, Radio Equipment Directive (RED) — required for all electronic products sold in the EU |
| **FCC** | United States | Electromagnetic interference (EMI) and radio frequency compliance — required for all electronic devices sold in the US |
| **UKCA** | United Kingdom | UK-specific product safety and EMC marking, required post-Brexit for goods placed on the GB market |
| **PSE** | Japan | Electrical appliance safety certification required by Japan's Electrical Appliance and Material Safety Act (DENAN) |
| **SAA** | Australia & New Zealand | Electrical safety certification recognized by the Regulatory Compliance Mark (RCM) framework |
| **BSCI** | Global (Retail Supply Chains) | Business Social Compliance Initiative audit — demonstrates ethical manufacturing practices; required by many European retailers |
| **ISO 9001** | Global | Quality management system certification; confirms consistent process controls across design, production, and delivery |

### Notes on Regional Certification

- **CE + UKCA**: If you are selling into both the EU and the UK, both marks are required; Topleo can supply products carrying both.
- **FCC**: Covers both intentional radiators (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth) and unintentional radiators (HDMI, USB). Topleo's FCC-certified models have passed testing for all integrated radios.
- **PSE**: Two categories exist in Japan — the diamond PSE mark (Class A, higher risk) and the circle PSE mark (Class B). Confirm the applicable category for your model with the Topleo compliance team.
- **BSCI**: Topleo's BSCI audit certification is available on request to satisfy ethical-sourcing requirements from retail buyers.

## Environmental Compliance

Environmental regulations govern the materials used inside every projector and the chemicals present in finished goods. Topleo's products are designed to comply with both major international frameworks, ensuring you can sell into regulated markets without material non-compliance risk.

---

### RoHS — Restriction of Hazardous Substances

**Directive:** EU Directive 2011/65/EU (RoHS 2), as amended by 2015/863/EU (RoHS 3)

RoHS restricts the use of specific hazardous materials in electrical and electronic equipment. Topleo products comply with RoHS restrictions on the following substances:

| Substance | Maximum Permitted Concentration |
|---|---|
| Lead (Pb) | 0.1% by weight |
| Mercury (Hg) | 0.1% by weight |
| Cadmium (Cd) | 0.01% by weight |
| Hexavalent Chromium (Cr VI) | 0.1% by weight |
| Polybrominated Biphenyls (PBB) | 0.1% by weight |
| Polybrominated Diphenyl Ethers (PBDE) | 0.1% by weight |
| Bis(2-ethylhexyl) phthalate (DEHP) | 0.1% by weight |
| Butyl benzyl phthalate (BBP) | 0.1% by weight |
| Dibutyl phthalate (DBP) | 0.1% by weight |
| Diisobutyl phthalate (DIBP) | 0.1% by weight |

RoHS compliance is a prerequisite for the CE mark and is required for sale in the EU, UK, and many other jurisdictions that have adopted equivalent legislation.

---

### REACH — Registration, Evaluation, Authorisation and Restriction of Chemicals

**Regulation:** EU Regulation (EC) No 1907/2006

REACH is the EU's comprehensive chemicals regulation. It places the burden of proof on manufacturers and importers to demonstrate that chemicals used in products are safe. Key obligations relevant to Topleo products:

- **SVHC Compliance** — Products are tested against the current ECHA Candidate List of Substances of Very High Concern (SVHCs). Topleo monitors ECHA Candidate List updates and re-evaluates component materials when new substances are added.
- **Article Compliance** — Finished projectors and their sub-assemblies are assessed as articles under REACH Article 33, ensuring SVHC concentrations remain below the 0.1% w/w threshold.
- **Supply Chain Communication** — Topleo provides REACH compliance documentation (including SVHC declarations) to OEM/ODM customers on request, supporting your own downstream obligations.

---

### Why Environmental Compliance Matters for Your Brand

- **Market Access** — RoHS and REACH compliance are legally required to place products on the EU and UK markets. Non-compliance can result in border seizure, product recalls, and significant fines.
- **Retail Requirements** — Major global retailers increasingly require REACH SVHC declarations as part of their supplier qualification process, independent of legal requirements.
- **Brand Protection** — Demonstrable environmental compliance reduces reputational risk and supports ESG reporting for publicly listed customers.
