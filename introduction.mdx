## 2026 Catalog at a Glance

The 2026 lineup spans **27 models** across five product families, covering everything from entry-level home-entertainment projectors to flagship Android 14 smart projectors with Google TV certification and Netflix licensing. Whether you need a white-label product ready for retail shelves or a fully custom platform built to your specifications, Topleo's catalog has a foundation for you.

### Key Technologies

- **LTPS LCD** — Low-Temperature Poly-Silicon LCD panels deliver superior brightness uniformity and long panel life compared to standard a-Si LCD.
- **DLP** — Digital Light Processing chipsets (P Series) for high-contrast, speckle-free images ideal for business and portable use.
- **Android 14** — Latest Android TV OS for access to a broad app ecosystem, regular security patches, and modern UI performance.
- **8K Video Decode** — Hardware-accelerated 8K decoding ensures smooth playback of high-resolution local and streamed content.
- **Google TV (GTV)** — Full Google TV experience with Google Assistant, Chromecast built-in, and the Google Play Store on select models.
- **Netflix Certification** — Native Netflix app at full 1080p resolution on certified models (TP300 NTV, TP350 NTV).
