Ordering from Topleo is straightforward whether you are buying stock models for resale or commissioning a fully customized OEM/ODM product. Topleo supports both paths through a structured process designed to minimize back-and-forth and get your units to market quickly. With PCBA samples available in as little as 24 hours and mass production completed in 7 working days, Topleo's supply chain is built for speed without sacrificing quality control. Every shipment leaves the factory only after a final QC inspection, and regional warehouses in the US and Poland mean North American and European buyers enjoy significantly shorter delivery windows.

## Lead Times at a Glance

| Stage | Timeline |
|---|---|
| PCBA Sample | 24 hours |
| Mass Production | 7 working days |
| Delivery (from regional warehouse) | Contact Topleo for current estimates |

## Shipping Options

Choose the shipping term that best fits your logistics setup and customs capabilities.

| Option | Who Pays Duties & Taxes | Who Handles Customs Clearance | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| **DDP** (Delivered Duty Paid) | Topleo / seller | Topleo handles on your behalf | Buyers who want a simple, all-in landed cost with no customs involvement |
| **DDU** (Delivered Duty Unpaid) | Buyer | Buyer or buyer's customs broker | Buyers with in-house logistics teams or preferential duty arrangements |

<Tip>
  Topleo maintains warehouses in the **United States** (for North American buyers) and **Poland** (for European buyers). Shipping from these regional hubs significantly reduces delivery time compared to standard ocean or air freight from Shenzhen. Contact the Topleo team for current delivery estimates to your region.
</Tip>

## Order Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Your Model(s)">
    Browse the Topleo catalog and identify the projector model(s) that fit your use case. If you need OEM/ODM customization, note your requirements at this stage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Topleo with Your Specifications">
    Reach out via the [Contact page](/sourcing/contact) with the model name(s), desired quantity, target market or region, and any OEM/ODM or certification requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Your Quotation">
    The Topleo sourcing team will respond with a detailed quotation — including unit pricing, tooling or customization costs if applicable, certification scope, and lead time — within **1 business day**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Order & Pay Deposit">
    Review and accept the quotation, sign the sales contract, and submit the agreed deposit to lock in your production slot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Production & Quality Control">
    Your order enters production. Every unit undergoes Topleo's standard QC process. For OEM/ODM orders, an approved sample serves as the quality benchmark throughout production.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Shipping">
    Completed units are shipped under your chosen Incoterm (DDP or DDU) from Shenzhen or a regional warehouse, with full tracking provided.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delivery">
    Your order arrives at the destination. DDP buyers receive fully cleared, duty-paid goods. DDU buyers coordinate final customs clearance through their own broker or logistics partner.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  All orders include a final QC inspection before shipment. Topleo will share an inspection report upon request.
</Note>
