## How to Reach Out

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify Your Model">
    Review the product catalog to find the projector model(s) that match your requirements. Visit the [Introduction & Catalog](/introduction) page to browse all available models.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Certifications Needed">
    Determine which regional certifications (CE, FCC, UKCA, etc.) apply to your target market. The [Certifications](/certifications) page provides a full breakdown by region.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact the Sourcing Team">
    Visit [www.topleo.com](https://www.topleo.com) and submit your inquiry through the website, or reach out to the sourcing team directly with your project details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Your Quotation">
    A Topleo sourcing specialist will review your requirements and send you a detailed quotation — including pricing, lead time, and certification scope — within **1 business day**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  To get the fastest response, include all key details in your first message: the model name(s) you're interested in, your target quantity, the destination market or region, any certifications required, and your OEM/ODM requirements if applicable. Complete inquiries are quoted significantly faster.
</Note>

## What to Include in Your Inquiry

When contacting Topleo, provide the following information to ensure a fast and accurate quotation:

- **Model name(s)** — the specific Topleo projector model(s) you are sourcing
- **Target quantity** — your expected order volume (initial and recurring if known)
- **Target market / region** — e.g., United States, EU, United Kingdom, Southeast Asia
- **Required certifications** — CE, FCC, UKCA, or other market-specific approvals
- **OEM / ODM requirements** — branding assets, firmware customizations, packaging specs, or custom hardware needs
