The TP400 is Topleo's compact, no-calibration-required 1080P projector, purpose-built for users who want to set it down and start watching without fussing over settings. With a 2.69" LTPS LCD panel, 650 ANSI lumens, a compact 0.8:1 throw ratio, and fully automatic focus and keystone correction, the TP400 makes 1080P projection genuinely effortless. It comes in a standard edition and a Google-certified GTV edition, so you can choose the right software ecosystem for your needs while enjoying identical optics and intelligent automation on both.

<Note>
  Both the TP400 and TP400 GTV include automatic focus and automatic keystone correction as standard, built-in hardware features. After you place the projector, neither requires any manual lens adjustment or geometry calibration — the unit handles it all at startup.
</Note>
