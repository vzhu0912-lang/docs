The HY260 is Topleo's standard full-HD portable projector, offering 1920×1080P resolution through a 2.69" LTPS LCD display. With 330 ANSI Lumens of brightness, a short 0.8:1 throw ratio, and built-in Ok Google voice assistant support, it gives you a versatile, room-filling image from as little as 0.5 metres away. Auto focus takes the guesswork out of setup, so you spend less time adjusting and more time watching.

<Tip>
  Every HY260 ships with a dedicated storage bag, keeping your projector and accessories protected between uses — a thoughtful inclusion that adds real everyday value.
</Tip>

> **Looking for more?** The [HY260 PRO](/models/hy260-pro) builds on this model with upgraded internals. If you need the next step up, check that page for a full comparison.
