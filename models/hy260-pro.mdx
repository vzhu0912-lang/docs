The HY260 PRO is Topleo's most travel-ready projector, blending smart home integration with genuine on-the-go portability. It ships with built-in Ok Google voice assistant support, so you can search content, adjust settings, and control playback entirely hands-free. An auto-focus mode means the image snaps to sharpness the moment you power on, without any manual tuning. And unlike most projectors in its class, the HY260 PRO comes bundled with a protective storage bag — making it as easy to pack for a weekend trip as it is to set up in your living room.

## Key Differentiators

- **Ok Google voice assistant** — hands-free voice control lets you search content, launch apps, and manage playback without touching a remote or the projector itself.
- **Auto-focus mode** — the HY260 PRO automatically calibrates focus on power-up, eliminating manual adjustments and getting you to your content faster.
- **Included storage bag** — a protective carry bag ships in the box, making this the most portable and travel-ready model in the Topleo lineup.
- **0.8:1 short-throw ratio** — project up to 130 inches from as close as 0.5 m, making the HY260 PRO a natural fit for smaller rooms and tight spaces.
