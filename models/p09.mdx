The P09 is Topleo's flagship pocket projector for users who need genuine portability without sacrificing image quality. Built around a 0.2" DLP DMD chip, it produces 800×480P resolution at 65 ANSI Lumens — notably brighter than comparable LCD pocket models — and its 4000mAh built-in battery means you can project for hours without ever looking for a power outlet. Add power bank input support and a generous 30–120 inch throw range across 1–5 M, and the P09 becomes a remarkably capable companion for travel, outdoor viewing, and spontaneous presentations.

<Tip>
  The 4000mAh built-in battery unlocks true cord-free projection — take the P09 to a campsite, a rooftop, a hotel room, or a backyard and enjoy up to a full movie runtime without needing any external power source. Combined with power bank input support, you can even extend playback by chaining an external battery pack.
</Tip>

## DLP vs LCD: Why It Matters

DLP (Digital Light Processing) projects images by reflecting light off millions of microscopic mirrors on the DMD chip. The result is higher contrast, more accurate colour, and a sharper pixel boundary compared to transmissive LCD panels at the same price tier. For a pocket projector used in varied lighting conditions, that extra contrast headroom makes a meaningful real-world difference.
