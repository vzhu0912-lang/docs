The HY320 Mini brings an accessible entry point to the Topleo projector lineup without sacrificing image quality. Its 2.69" LTPS LCD panel produces a clean 1280×720P picture at up to 300 ANSI Lumens, while the compact form factor makes it easy to carry in a bag or slide into a drawer. Covering projection sizes from 30 to 150 inches at distances of 1–4 M, the HY320 Mini handles everything from desktop gaming sessions to full living-room movie nights.

<Tip>
  The included lens cap is more than a basic accessory — it actively protects the optical element during storage and transit. For retail and consumer packaging, the lens cap adds a premium feel that resonates strongly with gift buyers and first-time projector owners.
</Tip>
