The TP900 is Topleo's ultra-short-throw 1080P projector, engineered for spaces where conventional projectors simply don't fit. With a 0.45:1 throw ratio, the TP900 can project a large, crisp image from less than a meter away — making it ideal for living rooms with limited depth, conference rooms with tables close to the wall, or any installation where you need maximum screen size in minimum distance. Automatic hexagonal trapezoidal correction, obstacle avoidance, and screen self-adaptation mean the TP900 sets itself up as quickly as you can place it.

<Tip>
  The TP900's minimum projection distance is just 0.55 M. At that distance you can already fill a screen well beyond typical TV sizes — making the TP900 one of the most space-efficient ways to get a cinematic image in a compact room.
</Tip>

## Short-Throw Placement

<Warning>
  Because the TP900 projects at a very steep angle over a short distance, the surface it sits on must be flat, level, and free of obstructions directly in front of the lens. Even a small object between the projector and the screen — or an uneven surface that tilts the unit — can cause significant image distortion that automatic correction cannot fully compensate for.
</Warning>
