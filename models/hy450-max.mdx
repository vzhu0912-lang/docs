The HY450 MAX is Topleo's flagship auto-everything projector, combining a 4.5" LTPS LCD panel with 1100 ANSI lumens of brightness and a complete suite of automatic setup features — focus, keystone correction, obstacle avoidance, and screen alignment — so you can go from box to big screen in seconds. Whether you choose the standard HY450 MAX or the Google-certified HY450 MAX GTV, you get the same powerful optics and intelligent automation in a compact form factor built for modern living rooms.

<Tip>
  At 1100 ANSI lumens, the HY450 MAX is bright enough to deliver a clear, vivid picture even in rooms with moderate ambient light. You don't need to black out your windows for a great viewing experience.
</Tip>

    ### Automatic Setup Features

    - **Auto Focus** — The HY450 MAX analyzes the projected image and sharpens focus automatically at power-on, eliminating the need for manual lens adjustment.
    - **Auto Hexagonal Keystone Correction** — Six-point trapezoidal correction runs automatically so your image is perfectly rectangular regardless of projector angle.
    - **Auto Obstacle Avoidance** — Built-in sensors detect objects in the projection path and adjust the image boundary in real time to avoid obstructions.
    - **Auto Screen Entry** — The projector identifies your screen or wall surface and aligns the projected image precisely within its borders.

## Projection Size

<Note>
  The HY450 MAX supports projection sizes from 40 to 200 inches. At the maximum 200-inch size, ensure your wall or screen surface is large enough and that ambient lighting is controlled for the best image quality at that scale.
</Note>

Your HY450 MAX ships with everything you need to start projecting immediately. Refer to the Quick Start Guide included in the packaging for step-by-step setup instructions, or visit [www.topleo.com](https://www.topleo.com) for setup guidance.
