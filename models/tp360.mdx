The Topleo TP360 family is a range of portable Android 14.0 smart projectors built for life on the move. Capable of decoding 8K video, each model packs a surprising amount of performance into a compact, travel-friendly form factor. All three models — the TP360, the TP360 Football, and the closely related HY300 F — share the same brilliant 1280×720P image from a 2.69" LTPS LCD display, a built-in HiFi speaker, and a short-throw lens that lets you fill a large screen even in tight spaces.

<Tip>
  The TP360 and TP360 Football feature a **0.8:1 short throw ratio**, while the HY300 F uses a **0.9:1 throw ratio** — both let you project a 100-inch image from well under 3 meters, making the whole family ideal for small rooms, dorm setups, or impromptu outdoor screenings.
</Tip>

## Specifications
