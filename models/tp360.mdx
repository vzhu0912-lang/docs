The Topleo TP360 is a portable smart projector built for life on the move. Powered by Android 14.0 and capable of decoding 8K video, it packs a surprising amount of performance into a compact, travel-friendly form factor. Whether you choose the standard TP360 or the eye-catching TP360 Football edition, you get the same brilliant 1280×720P image from a 2.69" LTPS LCD display, a built-in HiFi speaker, and a 0.8:1 short-throw lens that lets you fill a large screen even in tight spaces.

<Tip>
  The TP360's **0.8:1 short throw ratio** means you can project a 100-inch image from under 2 meters away — perfect for small rooms, dorm setups, or impromptu outdoor screenings where space is limited.
</Tip>
