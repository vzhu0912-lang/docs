The Topleo HY350 MAX is a feature-rich 1080P smart projector designed for effortless setup and stunning image quality right out of the box. Equipped with a large 3.5" LTPS LCD panel, 650 ANSI lumens of brightness, and a comprehensive suite of automatic image-correction tools, the HY350 MAX removes the guesswork from projection. Simply point it at your screen, power it on, and let the projector handle the rest. For users who want the full Google ecosystem baked in, the HY350 MAX GTV adds Google TV OS, Google Assistant, Chromecast built-in, and the Google Play Store.

<Note>
  The HY350 MAX's **hexagonal keystone correction** calculates and corrects distortion across six points rather than the standard four — delivering a noticeably more accurate, natural-looking rectangle on irregular surfaces or angled walls.
</Note>
