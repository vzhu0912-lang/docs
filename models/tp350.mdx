The Topleo TP350 stands out in the 1080P projector market with a capability rarely found at its price point: **15° left and right side projection**. While most projectors require direct front-facing placement, the TP350 lets you mount or position the unit at an angle — up to 15 degrees to either side — without sacrificing image quality. This makes it the most versatile and cost-effective option in the Topleo lineup for creative mounting setups, corner installations, and unconventional room layouts. Add a 3.5" LTPS LCD panel, 650 ANSI lumens of brightness, and auto/electric focus, and you have a projector that's as capable as it is flexible.

<Tip>
  The TP350's **15° side projection** is a game-changer for corner-mounted setups — instead of repositioning your furniture around the projector, you can mount the unit in a corner and angle the image toward your screen or wall without geometric distortion.
</Tip>

## Specifications

Both the TP350 and TP350 NTV share the following core hardware and optical specifications.

| Specification         | Value                  |
|-----------------------|------------------------|
| Display Technology    | 3.5" LTPS LCD          |
| Native Resolution     | 1920×1080P             |
| Brightness            | 650 ANSI Lumens        |
| Throw Ratio           | 1.16:1                 |
| Projection Size       | 40–120 inch            |
| Projection Distance   | 1–4 M                  |
| Lamp Life             | ≥ 50,000 Hrs           |
| Focus                 | Auto / Electric Focus  |
| Side Projection       | ±15° (left and right)  |
| Video Decode          | Up to 8K               |

<Note>
  Side projection angles beyond ±15° may result in image distortion not fully correctable by keystone adjustment. For best results, keep placement within the supported angular range.
</Note>
