The HY450 PRO is Topleo's flagship short-throw projector, purpose-built for rooms where space is at a premium. Its optical off-axis design automatically tilts the projected image upward, so you can place the unit flat on a shelf or table and fill your screen without any manual angle adjustment. Paired with a 0.6:1 throw ratio and 900 ANSI lumens of brightness, the HY450 PRO delivers sharp, vivid 1080P visuals even in well-lit environments — all from a remarkably short distance.

<Tip>
  The optical off-axis design is perfect for shelf or low table placement. Because the image automatically projects upward, you don't need a ceiling mount or an adjustable stand — just set the HY450 PRO down and point it at your wall or screen.
</Tip>

<Note>
  The HY450 PRO supports projection sizes from **40 to 200 inches**, giving you the flexibility to fill a small bedroom wall or a large living-room screen from as close as 0.55 m away.
</Note>

## Key Differentiators

- **Optical off-axis design** — the projected image tilts upward automatically from a flat, tabletop position, eliminating the need for manual tilt adjustment or ceiling mounting.
- **0.6:1 throw ratio** — project a large image from an extremely short distance, making the HY450 PRO ideal for compact rooms and tight spaces.
- **900 ANSI lumens** — bright enough to watch comfortably in ambient light without having to fully darken the room.
- **Auto / electric focus** — sharp focus is achieved automatically or via motorized electric control, so you spend less time tweaking and more time watching.
