The HY300 Plus refines the entry-level 720P experience with one standout upgrade: an electronic focus mechanism that lets you dial in a perfectly sharp image without ever touching the lens. Paired with a 2.69" LTPS LCD display, 310 ANSI Lumens of output, and an impressively wide 40–150 inch projection range, it gives everyday users a comfortable, precise setup experience that feels a step above basic manual-focus competitors. Whether you're mounting it on a shelf or repositioning it between rooms, the HY300 Plus consistently delivers crisp results.

## Why Choose Electronic Focus?

Unlike manual focus, the HY300 Plus's electronic focus system responds to button presses with incremental, repeatable adjustments. You get consistent results every time you power on the unit, and there is no physical contact with the lens optics — meaning less wear and a longer-lasting, cleaner image over the projector's lifetime.
