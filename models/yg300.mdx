The YG300 is the most compact and affordable projector in the entire Topleo lineup, purpose-built for buyers who want the projector experience at the lowest possible price point. Its 1.8" LTPS LCD panel produces a 480×272P image, and at just 126.5×86×48 mm it fits comfortably in the palm of your hand. Covering projection sizes from 32 to 120 inches at distances between 1 and 3.5 M, the YG300 is a genuine projector — not a toy — but it is best understood as an introductory device suited for casual, low-ambient-light environments.

<Warning>
  At 20 ANSI Lumens, the YG300 is designed exclusively for **dark room use**. In rooms with normal ambient lighting, the projected image will appear dim and washed out. Always use the YG300 with lights off or significantly dimmed for the best viewing experience.
</Warning>

<Note>
  At 126.5×86×48 mm and a featherweight build, the YG300's physical dimensions make it an ideal candidate for the **gift and novelty market**. It fits neatly in standard retail boxes, ships economically, and delivers a genuine "wow" moment when unwrapped — a winning combination for seasonal gifting, promotional bundles, and consumer electronics gift sets.
</Note>

## Who Is the YG300 For?

The YG300 is ideal for children's bedroom movie nights, dorm-room use, travel entertainment in blacked-out hotel rooms, and anyone taking their first step into home projection. It is also a compelling choice for retailers building gift bundles where a low unit cost and high perceived value are both important.
