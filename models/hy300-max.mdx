The HY300 MAX is Topleo's Google-certified portable projector, offering a 720P HD image with 390 ANSI lumens of brightness in a compact, go-anywhere form factor. Built around a 2.69" LTPS LCD panel and a 0.8:1 throw ratio, it delivers projection sizes from 35 to 135 inches across distances of 0.5 to 3 meters — making it equally at home as a bedroom projector, a travel companion, or a fixed-installation unit in a smaller space. Both the HY300 MAX and HY300 MAX GTV share identical hardware specifications; the GTV edition adds the full Google TV software experience on top of the same reliable optics.

## Manual Focus

<Tip>
  Manual focus is an advantage in fixed-installation scenarios. Once you dial in the focus for your specific throw distance, it stays locked — there's no automatic refocus cycle at startup that could shift between sessions. If you're mounting the HY300 MAX in a dedicated home theater nook, bedroom shelf, or conference room ceiling bracket, manual focus gives you a stable, set-and-forget result.
</Tip>

To set focus, adjust the focus ring on the lens until the projected image appears sharp. You only need to repeat this process if you move the projector to a new position or change the projection distance.
