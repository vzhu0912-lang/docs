The P11 delivers the same core DLP projection experience as its sibling the P09, wrapped in a more compact physical package. At 145×105×84 mm it is noticeably smaller than the P09, making it the better choice when you want the sharpest possible image in the tightest possible form factor. Its 0.2" DMD chip outputs 800×480P at 65 ANSI Lumens, a 3000mAh built-in battery keeps it running without a cable, and power bank input support extends that runtime whenever you need it. For travellers and commuters where every cubic centimetre counts, the P11 strikes an excellent balance.

<Tip>
  Both the P09 and P11 use DLP technology — specifically the same 0.2" DMD chip — which gives them a clear advantage over LCD pocket projectors in the same price bracket. Expect sharper colour contrast, reduced motion blur, and a crisper image boundary that holds up better when projecting on textured or off-white surfaces.
</Tip>

> **Need more battery?** The [P09](/models/p09) carries a 4000mAh battery in a slightly larger body. If extended cordless runtime matters more to you than a compact footprint, the P09 is the stronger choice.
