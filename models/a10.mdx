The A10 is Topleo's most convenient everyday projector, designed for users who want effortless setup without ever touching the unit itself. Its electric focus system is operated entirely through the included remote, so you can fine-tune image sharpness from the comfort of your couch — or from across a large room. With a generous 30–200 inch projection range and a compact 2.69" LTPS LCD panel delivering native 1080P resolution, the A10 adapts to virtually any screen size or viewing environment you have in mind.

<Tip>
  The remote-controlled electric focus makes the A10 an excellent choice for ceiling-mounted installations. Because you never need to physically reach the projector to adjust sharpness, you can lock it in place once and refine focus at any time using only the remote.
</Tip>

## Key Differentiators

- **Electric remote focus** — adjust image sharpness from anywhere in the room without touching the projector, ideal for permanent or hard-to-reach installations.
- **30–200 inch projection range** — one of the widest available in this class, giving you the flexibility to go from a compact 30-inch display to a full 200-inch cinematic projection.
- **Native 1080P resolution** — full HD clarity on a 2.69" LTPS LCD panel, producing sharp detail across the entire projection surface.
- **≥50,000-hour lamp life** — designed for years of daily use before any lamp maintenance is required.
