The Topleo TP300 proves that full 1080P performance doesn't require a large, bulky device. As the most compact 1080P LCD projector in the Topleo lineup, the TP300 squeezes a 2.69" LTPS LCD display, 450 ANSI lumens of brightness, and automatic focus into a chassis small enough to carry anywhere. Its 0.8:1 short-throw lens covers 40 to 150 inches from as little as 0.6 meters away, making it an ideal choice for apartments, offices, and any space where distance is at a premium. The TP300 NTV variant takes things further with official Netflix certification, enabling the native Netflix app at full 1080P resolution.

<Note>
  The TP300 is the **smallest 1080P LCD projector** in the Topleo lineup — delivering full high-definition resolution in a form factor typically associated with lower-resolution portable projectors.
</Note>
