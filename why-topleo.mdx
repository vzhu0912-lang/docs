## OEM / ODM Capabilities

Topleo supports two engagement models to fit your sourcing strategy:

| Mode | What You Get | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **OEM** | Build to your exact hardware and software specification; Topleo manufactures under your brand | Buyers with an established product design seeking a reliable manufacturing partner |
| **ODM** | Select and customize an existing Topleo reference design — hardware, branding, firmware, packaging | Buyers who want proven hardware with custom branding and faster time-to-market |

- **Hardware** — Custom PCB layouts, sensor integration, port configuration, optics selection
- **Branding** — Logo, color, packaging design, startup animation, default wallpaper
- **Software** — Custom launcher UI, pre-loaded apps, region-specific content, OTA update server
- **Certification Support** — Topleo's compliance team assists with CE, FCC, UKCA, PSE, SAA, and platform (GTV, Netflix) certification processes
